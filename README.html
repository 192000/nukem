About Nuke'm

Nuke'm is a lightweight and easy-to-use chrome extension that clears basic and advanced Google Chrome browsing data.
Nuke'm deletes LocalSotrage, IndexedDB, Downloads (Optional), History, All Cache,
Cookies (Optional), FileSystems, FormData, PluginData, Passwords (Optional), Tabs (Optional),
Sever Bound Certificates, Bookmarks (Optional), and WebSQL Data.
Clearing of this browsing data is preformed with a simple click of the extension,
and it can be configured in the settings menu.

NEW: AUTO-NUKE HISTORY
Nuke'm can now clear history automatically! Just navigate to the Options page, and enable Auto-Nuke History.

AUTO-NUKE HISTORY NOTICE:
If Auto-Nuke History is not working for you, please go to Google Chrome's settings,
navigate to Sync, disable Sync Everything, and disable the History option I have circled in red:





Requirements

Operating System: Chrome OS, Mac, Windows, and Linux
Browser: Google Chrome

Usage

Simply click the extension icon located in the top right after installation,
and all browsing data and configured browsing data will be cleaned.
If you would like to change how Nuke'm operates, right click on the extension's icon
and from the dropdown click on "Options".
